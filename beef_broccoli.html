Beef and Broccoli Recipe!

Description

Asian-inspired Beef and Broccoli is high in protein and flavor. This is great if your looking for something different.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of flank steak
  • 3 tablespoons of cornstarch (divided)
  • 3 tablespoons of water
  • 3 tabelspoons
  • 2 tablespoons of grated fresh ginger
  • 1/2 cup low-sodium soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of sesame oil
  • 1/2 teaspoons of ground black pepper
  • 3 tablespoons of brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of minced garlic
  • 4 cups of broccoli florets
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil, divided
  • Cooked white rice and sesame seeds for serving

Directions

Step 1

Cut the flank stak against the grain into slices 1/4 inches thick.

Step 2

In a large bowl, combine 2 tablespoons of cornstarch and 3 tablespoons of water together. Add the sliced flank steak and mix to coat evenely, set aside after your done

Step 3

In a small bowl, mix the ginger, soy sauce, sesame oil, pepper, brown sugar, garlic, and remaining cornstarch together using the serving sizes from the ingredients list.

Step 4

Cut the broccoli florets into evenly sized pieces

Step 5

Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large skillet set over medium-high heat. Add the broccoli and coo for about 4-5 minutes. ounce cooked, transfer the broccoli to a small bowl and set aside

Step 6

Add the remaining oil and the flank steak strips to the skillet. Cook over medium-high heat until the steak has cooked evenly, about 3-4 minutes.

Step 7

Pour the soy sauce mixture into hte skillet and stir well

Step 8

Add the cooked broccoli and stir until evenly heated throughout the meal. Serve with rice and sesame seeds and then enjoy!
