Quesadilla and Jalepeno Ranch Dip Recipe!

Who doesn't love Quesdillas!!

Food doesn't get much more fun than this cheese quesadilla with jalepeno ranch, and we hope that you have as much fun making this easy recipe as we did eating it.

Ingredients for the Quesadilla

  • 10 flour tortillas
  • 1 cup of grated mozzarella
  • 1 cup of grated pepper jack cheese
  • 2 large avocodo, thinly slice
  • 1/2 cup of minced green onions

Ingredients for the Jalepeno Ranch

  • 1/2 cup of mayonnaise
  • 1/2 cup of sour cream
  • 1 tablespoon of onion powder
  • 1 tablespoon of garlic powder
  • 1/2 tablespoon of salt
  • 1/4 cup of finely minced cilantro
  • 1/2 cup of drained picled jalapenos
  • 2 tablespoons of lime juice

Directions for Quesadilla's

Step 1

Preheat the oven to 425 degrees Farenheit

Step 2

On a large greased baking sheet arrange six tortillas around the perimeter so that half of each tortilla is hanging over the edge. Fill the middle of the baking sheet with two or more tortillas.

Step 3

Top with grated cheese followed by avocados and green onions

Fold the overhanging tortilla into the center to cover the filling and finish off with two more tortillas over the center. Generously brush or spray tortillas with oil and place a second (oiled underneath) baking sheet on top of tortillas to keetortillas flat while baking. Bake for 20 minutes, followed by 5 mintues uncovered or until golden and crispy.

Step 4

Cut quesadilla into large squares and serve dipped into jalapeno ranch

Direction for Jalepeno Dip

Step 1

Blend all of the ingrediets for Jalepeno dip in a blender or food processor.

Step 2

Enjoy your delicious meal!
